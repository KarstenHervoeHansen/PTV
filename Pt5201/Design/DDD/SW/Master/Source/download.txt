Downloading the SW to the PT5201:

PT5201.HEX should be downloaded from address 0hex

AES_SPG.HEX should be downloaded from address 40000hex

5201SND1.HEX should be downloaded from address 4A000hex

5201DATA.HEX should be downloaded from address 50000hex

This may require that the generated hex files are manually altered in order for
the boot loader to recognize the load address.
